Gulab Jamun

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Syrup: Boil sugar, water, cardamom for 5–7 mins. Add rose water, keep warm..
  2. Dough: Mix dry ingredients, add ghee, then milk to form soft dough. Rest 10 mins.
  3. Shape: Make smooth, crack-free balls.
  4. Fry: Deep fry on low-medium heat until golden brown.
  5. Soak: Add to warm syrup. Let soak 1–2 hours.

Barfi Jamun

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Make Sugar Syrup: Boil sugar + water + cardamom for 5–7 mins. Add rose water.
  2. Prepare Dough: Mix milk powder, maida, baking soda, ghee, then milk. Knead soft dough. Rest 10 mins.
  3. Make Barfi: Mix khoya, sugar, cardamom. Cook slightly. Let it cool
  4. Soak: Add to warm syrup and soak for 1 hour.
